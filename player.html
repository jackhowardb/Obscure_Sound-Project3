I Called Your Name • Tape Tension    

“I Called Your Name” is a track out from Tape Tension, showing a lush and
bouncy electro-pop charm that glistens and hooks. “I Called Your Name”
marks the third and final single from Tape Tension’s upcoming second full-length album, Questions In Balance. Serene, spacious vocals play amidst pulsing synth effervescence; the percussive lead-in to the twinkling around 01:08 plays with a charming allure. “I Called Your Name” is serene, twinkling ear candy from Tape Tension.
